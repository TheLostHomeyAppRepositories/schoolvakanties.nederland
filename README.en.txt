School Holidays in the Netherlands – Easily Check if It's a Holiday!

Want to know if there’s a school holiday today, yesterday, or tomorrow in the Netherlands? With this Homey app, you can quickly check if there’s a holiday in your region and find out which holiday it is.

What can you do here?

- See if it’s a holiday today, yesterday, or tomorrow in Region North, Region Central, or Region South.
- Get details on specific holidays such as Autumn Break, Christmas Break, Spring Break, May Break, and Summer Break.
- Manage your own holidays: add custom holidays or remove holidays that are not relevant to you.
- View a complete overview of all school holidays by region in the Netherlands.

Example use case:
- Automation: Suppose your morning alarm goes off. If it's not a school holiday, let the alarm play morning music. If it’s Christmas Break, you can choose festive holiday tunes instead.
With this tool, you can easily stay informed about school holidays and adjust your plans accordingly!
